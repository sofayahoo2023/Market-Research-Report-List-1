<p><p>The Whipping Agent market consists of two main types: powdered whipping agent and liquid whipping agent. They are easier to measure and store compared to liquid whipping agents. Liquid whipping agents, on the other hand, are in liquid form and are usually added directly to the mixture. They are convenient and provide a smoother texture to whipped products. Both types serve the same purpose of enhancing the texture and stability of various culinary creations.</p></p>
