<p><p>The industrial diaphragm valves market consists of three main types: manual, pneumatic, and motorized valves. Manual diaphragm valves are manually operated by turning a handle or lever. Pneumatic diaphragm valves use air pressure to control the valve's opening and closing, offering faster and more precise control. Motorized diaphragm valves are operated by an electric motor, providing automated control and allowing for remote operation. These different types cater to various needs and requirements in different industrial applications, providing flexibility, efficiency, and ease of operation.</p></p>
