<p><strong>The Rain Light Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Type</li><li>Infrared Type</li><li>Resistive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rain light sensors are electronic devices that detect rain and automatically activate the windshield wipers on a vehicle. There are three main types of rain light sensors available in the market. Capacitive type sensors use electrical conductivity to detect raindrops on the windshield, while infrared type sensors utilize beams of infrared light to detect the presence of rain. On the other hand, resistive type sensors measure changes in resistance caused by water droplets on the windshield. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different customer requirements and preferences.</p></p>
