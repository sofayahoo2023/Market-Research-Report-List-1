<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The multi-touch sensing market can be categorized into two types: opaque multi-touch and transparent multi-touch. Opaque multi-touch refers to touch sensing technologies that are placed on opaque surfaces, such as computer screens or touchpads. These technologies detect touch inputs through pressure or capacitive sensors, allowing users to interact with the devices by tapping or swiping. On the other hand, transparent multi-touch technologies are designed for surfaces like glass or transparent screens. These technologies use specialized sensors or optical methods to detect touch inputs, enabling users to have interactive experiences while maintaining visibility through the transparent surface.</p></p>
