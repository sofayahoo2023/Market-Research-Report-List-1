<p><p>Polyurethane concrete sealers are widely used to protect and enhance the appearance of concrete surfaces. There are two main types of polyurethane concrete sealers: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based sealers are environmentally friendly, have low VOC content, and are easy to clean up. They offer good adhesion and UV resistance but may require multiple coats. On the other hand, solvent-based sealers provide excellent durability and protection against harsh environments. They typically require just one coat but have higher VOC content and are more difficult to clean up.</p></p>
