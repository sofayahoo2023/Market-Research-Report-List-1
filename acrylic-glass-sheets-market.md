<p><p>The acrylic glass sheets market is divided into two main types: extruded acrylic sheet and cast acrylic sheet. </p><p>Extruded acrylic sheets are made by continuously pushing the material through a die, resulting in a smooth and consistent surface with uniform thickness. They are cost-effective, lightweight, and have high impact resistance, making them suitable for various applications like signage, displays, and lighting. </p><p>On the other hand, cast acrylic sheets are manufactured using a casting process, resulting in a higher level of optical clarity and better resistance to scratching and chemicals. They are often used in more high-end applications such as architectural glazing, aquariums, and automotive parts.</p></p>
