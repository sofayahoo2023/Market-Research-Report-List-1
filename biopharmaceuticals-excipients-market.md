<p><p>The biopharmaceuticals excipients market comprises various types of excipients used in the production of biopharmaceutical products. Sorbitol is another sugar alcohol used as a stabilizer and sweetener. Sucrose is a disaccharide that stabilizes proteins and enhances stability. Dextrose, a simple sugar, is a bulking agent. Starch is a polysaccharide used as a binder. Other excipients include polymers, buffers, acids, bases, and antioxidants.</p></p>
