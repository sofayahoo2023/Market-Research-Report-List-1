<p><p>PCM (Pre-Coated Metal) steel sheets are widely used in the home appliance market. There are four main types of PCM steel sheets: Hot Dip Galvanized Plate, Hot Dip Aluminum Zinc Plate, Galvanized Steel Sheet, and Cold Rolled Plate. Hot Dip Galvanized Plate is coated with a layer of zinc for corrosion resistance. Hot Dip Aluminum Zinc Plate has a coating of aluminum and zinc for enhanced durability. Galvanized Steel Sheet is coated with zinc to prevent rust. Cold Rolled Plate is produced by repeatedly passing the steel through rollers to improve its strength and surface finish.</p></p>
