<p><p>The standalone smart thermostats market can be categorized into two types: wired and wireless. Wired smart thermostats are directly connected to the HVAC system using electrical wiring, allowing for a more stable and reliable connection. They require professional installation but offer advanced features and integration with other smart home devices. On the other hand, wireless smart thermostats use wireless technology such as Wi-Fi to connect to the HVAC system, providing flexibility in placement and easier installation. They can be controlled remotely through smartphone apps and offer a seamless user experience.</p></p>
