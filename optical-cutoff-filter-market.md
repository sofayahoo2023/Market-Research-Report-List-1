<p><strong>The Optical Cutoff Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shortpass Filter</li><li>Longpass Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Cutoff Filter market encompasses various types, including shortpass and longpass filters. Shortpass filters allow shorter wavelengths of light to pass through while blocking longer ones. They are widely used in applications such as fluorescence microscopy and spectroscopy. Longpass filters, on the other hand, permit longer wavelengths to pass and restrict shorter ones. They find applications in light pollution control, astronomy, and spectrophotometry. The different market types cater to specific wavelength requirements, allowing for precise manipulation of light in numerous industries.</p></p>
