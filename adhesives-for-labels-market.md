<p><p>The adhesives for labels market consists of two primary types: permanent adhesive and removable adhesive. Permanent adhesive labels are designed to permanently stick to surfaces, providing long-lasting adhesion that cannot be easily removed without causing damage. These are commonly used for branding, packaging, or labeling items that require a strong bond. On the other hand, removable adhesive labels offer a temporary bond, allowing users to easily peel and remove the label without leaving behind any residue or damage. Removable adhesive labels are widely used for temporary pricing, promotions, or labeling certain products.</p></p>
