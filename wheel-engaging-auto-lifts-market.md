<p><p>Wheel engaging auto lifts are used in the automotive industry to lift vehicles off the ground for various purposes. Two-post lifts are common types with two symmetrical posts, allowing vehicles to be lifted from the frame for maintenance or repairs. Four-post lifts have four supporting posts and are mainly used for storage or longer-term repairs. Scissor lifts feature a pantograph mechanism and are mostly found in smaller garages or workshops. Other market variations include in-ground lifts, mobile column lifts, and low-rise lifts, each designed for specific vehicle lifting needs.</p></p>
