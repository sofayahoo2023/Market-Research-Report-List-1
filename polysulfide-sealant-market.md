<p><p>The polysulfide sealant market comprises two main types: Two Component Polysulfide Sealant and Multi Component Polysulfide Sealant. Two Component Polysulfide Sealant is formulated by mixing two separate parts, typically a base and a curing agent, which then cure to form a strong adhesive. On the other hand, Multi Component Polysulfide Sealant consists of more than two components combined to offer enhanced properties and particular applications. These variants are widely used in industries like construction, automotive, aerospace, and marine for sealing joints and preventing leakage.</p></p>
