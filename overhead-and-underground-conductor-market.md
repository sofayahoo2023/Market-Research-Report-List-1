<p><p>Overhead and underground conductor markets refer to the different types of cables used for electrical transmission and distribution. AAC (All Aluminium Conductor) consists of stranded aluminum wires and is lightweight, suitable for overhead power lines. AAAC (All Aluminium Alloy Conductor) is similar, but with enhanced strength for higher tension applications. ACSR (Aluminium Conductor, Steel Reinforced) combines aluminum and steel strands for increased strength, commonly used for long-distance transmission lines. ACAR (Aluminium Conductor, Alloy Reinforced) is a variant of ACSR, utilizing aluminum alloy strands for improved conductivity. Other types of conductors exist, but these are the main ones.</p></p>
