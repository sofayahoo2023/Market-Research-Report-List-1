<p><p>Solar PV power, also known as solar photovoltaic power, is a renewable energy technology that converts sunlight into electricity. When sunlight strikes these panels, the photons in the sunlight energize the electrons in the semiconductor material, generating an electric current.</p><p>The global solar PV power market has witnessed significant growth in recent years, driven by the increasing demand for clean and sustainable energy sources. Firstly, there is a growing adoption of solar PV systems in the residential sector as homeowners aim to reduce their carbon footprint and save on energy costs. Additionally, there is a shift towards floating solar PV power plants, which are installed on water bodies, offering benefits like land conservation and increased efficiency due to water cooling.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing technological advancements like the integration of energy storage systems with solar PV power plants. This allows for better utilization of solar energy, as excess energy can be stored and used during non-sunlight hours, addressing the intermittent nature of solar power generation.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a regional shift, with China leading the global solar PV power market followed by countries like the United States, Japan, and India. These countries are investing heavily in solar PV power infrastructure and capacity expansion.</p><p>In conclusion, the solar PV power market is expected to grow at a significant rate in the forecast period, driven by policy support, declining costs, and technological advancements.
