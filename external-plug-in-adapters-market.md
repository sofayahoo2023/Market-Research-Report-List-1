<p><p>The external plug-in adapters market comprises three main types: AC/AC, AC/DC, and DC/DC. AC/AC adapters convert alternating current (AC) to another form of AC power, making them suitable for devices like voltage converters or audio equipment. AC/DC adapters convert AC power to direct current (DC), commonly used for small electronic devices such as laptops or smartphones. DC/DC adapters, on the other hand, convert DC power from one voltage level to another, often used for applications like battery charging or power supply for industrial equipment.</p></p>
