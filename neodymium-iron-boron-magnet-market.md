<p><strong>The Neodymium Iron Boron Magnet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonded NdFeB</li><li>Sintered NdFeB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Neodymium Iron Boron (NdFeB) Magnets are classified into two main types: Bonded NdFeB and Sintered NdFeB. </p><p>Bonded NdFeB magnets are made by mixing NdFeB powder with a binder and then compressing/aligning the mixture in a magnetic field. These magnets offer complex shapes, improved corrosion resistance, and lower magnetic properties compared to Sintered NdFeB magnets.</p><p>Sintered NdFeB magnets are produced through a process of compacting powdered NdFeB material into a desired shape and then sintering it at high temperatures. These magnets possess high magnetic strength, excellent performance at elevated temperatures, and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
