<p><p>Twin-screw extruder gearboxes can be classified into two types: solid shaft and hollow shaft. In a solid shaft gearbox, both the input and output shafts are made of solid materials, providing a strong and rigid structure. On the other hand, a hollow shaft gearbox has a hollow center in its input and output shafts, which allows for the passage of materials or cooling fluids. Both types have their specific applications, depending on factors such as required torque, speed, and materials being processed.</p></p>
