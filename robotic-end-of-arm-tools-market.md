Mechanical tools rely on mechanisms like gears and motors to perform tasks and are generally robust and versatile. Pneumatical tools use compressed air to generate force and are ideal for applications that require quick and repetitive movements. These tools provide diverse options for applications in the robotic industry.</p></p>
