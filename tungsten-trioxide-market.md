<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The tungsten trioxide market can be classified into two types - the Calcium Tungstate Method and the Secondary Ammonium Tungstate Method. In the Calcium Tungstate Method, raw materials like tungsten concentrates are treated with calcium oxide to produce calcium tungstate. This compound is then converted into tungsten trioxide. On the other hand, the Secondary Ammonium Tungstate Method involves the conversion of tungsten scraps into ammonium paratungstate, which is further converted into tungsten trioxide. Both methods are used to produce tungsten trioxide for various industrial applications.</p></p>
