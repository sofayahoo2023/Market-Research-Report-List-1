<p><p>The Light Vehicle Steering Systems market includes different types of steering systems such as Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), Electronic Power Steering (EPS), and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS). HPS uses hydraulic pressure to assist the driver in steering, helping in smoother and easier maneuverability. EPS, on the other hand, relies on an electric motor to provide steering assistance, resulting in better fuel efficiency and improved control. EHPS combines the features of both systems, providing the benefits of hydraulic power and electronic control, leading to enhanced steering performance and adaptability.</p></p>
