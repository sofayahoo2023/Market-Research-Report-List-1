<p><p>Nickel recycling market types include primary nickel and secondary nickel markets. In the primary nickel market, nickel is produced from newly mined ore and sold as a raw material. On the other hand, the secondary nickel market involves the recycling and repurposing of nickel-containing materials, such as scrap metal, electric vehicle batteries, and stainless steel waste. This promotes sustainability and reduces the need for extensive mining activities. The secondary nickel market supports a circular economy by reintroducing nickel into the production cycle through recycling processes.</p></p>
