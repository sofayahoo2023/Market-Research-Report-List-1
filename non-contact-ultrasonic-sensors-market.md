<p><p>Non-contact ultrasonic sensors are widely used in various industries for distance measurement, object detection, and monitoring applications. These sensors come in two main market types: Open Type and Sealed Type. </p><p>Open Type ultrasonic sensors have exposed transducers and are suitable for applications where the environment permits direct contact with the sensor. They are cost-effective and provide good sensitivity. </p><p>Sealed Type ultrasonic sensors are hermetically sealed and protected from environmental factors such as dust, moisture, and temperature variations. They are ideal for harsh environments or situations where direct contact is not possible or desired. Sealed Type sensors offer increased durability and reliability.</p></p>
