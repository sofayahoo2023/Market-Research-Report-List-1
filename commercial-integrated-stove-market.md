<p><p>The commercial integrated stove market encompasses various types, including deep well type and side suction type. Deep well type stoves feature a design that allows a cooking vessel to be placed in a well, which facilitates heat transfer and enhances cooking efficiency. On the other hand, side suction type stoves are designed with suction features on the sides, enabling fumes and smoke to be efficiently extracted during cooking. These different market types offer distinct benefits and cater to different commercial cooking needs.</p></p>
