There are two main types of glass-free 3D displays: Parallax Barrier Technology and Lenticular Display Technology. Parallax Barrier Technology uses a barrier layer to separate the left and right eye views, creating a 3D effect. Lenticular Display Technology uses an array of lenses to direct different images to each eye, creating the illusion of depth. Both types of displays offer immersive 3D viewing experiences without the use of glasses.</p></p>
