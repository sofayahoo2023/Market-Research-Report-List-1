<p><p>Chemical resistant coatings are used to protect surfaces from damage or corrosion caused by exposure to chemicals. The market for chemical resistant coatings can be classified into different types based on the solvent used in the coating formulation. Solvent-borne coatings are those in which the solvent evaporates during the drying process, leaving behind a protective film. Water-borne coatings, on the other hand, use water as the solvent. Other types of chemical resistant coatings include powder coatings, high solids coatings, and UV-curable coatings, among others. These coatings provide different benefits and are suitable for various applications depending on the specific requirements.</p></p>
