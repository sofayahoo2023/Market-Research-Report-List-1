<p><p>Control choke valves are used in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and power plants to regulate flow rates and pressure in pipelines. The market is divided into two types: fixed choke valves and adjustable choke valves. Fixed choke valves have a predetermined orifice size, providing a constant flow rate. Adjustable choke valves, on the other hand, offer the flexibility to change the orifice size manually or automatically, allowing for precise flow control. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining operational efficiency and safety in fluid transportation systems.</p></p>
