<p><p>The automotive idle air control valve market consists of three main types: Pulse Solenoid Valve Type, Rotary Solenoid Valve Type, and Stepping Motor Type. </p><p>Pulse solenoid valve type controls the airflow by regulating the opening and closing of a solenoid-operated valve, allowing pulses of air to pass through. </p><p>Rotary solenoid valve type utilizes a rotating solenoid mechanism to control the airflow, precisely adjusting the valve opening to regulate the amount of air entering the engine. </p><p>Stepping motor type uses a small motor to control the valve's position incrementally, allowing for smoother and more precise control of the airflow into the engine.</p></p>
