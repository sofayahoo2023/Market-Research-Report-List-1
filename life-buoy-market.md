<p><p>The life buoy market consists of different types of life-saving devices, including horseshoe and ring buoys. A horseshoe buoy is shaped like a U and is commonly used in watercrafts or ships for quick rescue operations, as it can be easily thrown overboard to a person in distress. On the other hand, a ring buoy is circular and is typically found near swimming pools or beaches for quick access in case of emergencies. Both types ensure the safety of individuals in water by providing buoyancy and acting as a flotation device.</p></p>
