<p><p>Automotive valves play a crucial role in regulating the flow of fluids or gases within a vehicle's engine system. The market for automotive valves can be segmented into various types. Solenoid valves control the flow of fluid using an electromagnetic coil. EGR valves redirect exhaust gas to the intake manifold for emission control. Brake combination valves help maintain brake pressure in braking systems. AT control valves regulate the transmission fluid pressure. Engine valves control the intake and exhaust of gases within the combustion chamber. These valves contribute to the overall functioning and efficiency of an automobile.</p></p>
