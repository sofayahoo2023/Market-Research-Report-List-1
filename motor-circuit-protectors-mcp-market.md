<p><p>Motor Circuit Protectors (MCP) are devices used to protect motors from electrical faults. There are two main types of MCPs: DC Motor Protection Circuit Breakers (MPCBs) and AC Motor Protection Circuit Breakers (MPCBs). DC MPCBs are specifically designed to guard against faults in direct current (DC) motor circuits. On the other hand, AC MPCBs are used to protect alternating current (AC) motor circuits. Both types of MPCBs provide vital protection by automatically interrupting the electrical supply when faults such as short circuits or overloads occur, preventing damage to motors and ensuring safe operation.</p></p>
