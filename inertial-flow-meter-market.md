On the other hand, a Gas Inertial Flow Meter is designed to measure the flow rate of gases in applications such as natural gas pipelines, HVAC systems, and biogas plants. Both types of meters utilize inertia and Coriolis forces to accurately measure flow rates without the need for moving parts or external power sources.</p></p>
