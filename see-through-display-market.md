<p><p>The See-through Display market consists of two main types: LED and LCD displays. LED displays, which stands for Light Emitting Diode, use tiny light-emitting diodes to produce the desired display output. LED displays offer high brightness and contrast, making them suitable for outdoor applications. On the other hand, LCD displays, or Liquid Crystal Displays, utilize liquid crystals that selectively allow light to pass through them, creating the visual display. LCD displays are known for their thin profile and energy efficiency, making them popular for various devices such as smartphones and televisions.</p></p>
