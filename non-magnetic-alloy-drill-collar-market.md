<p><p>The non-magnetic alloy drill collar market consists of two types - spiral and slick. Spiral drill collars are designed with spiral grooves on their outer surface, which provide improved mud flow and prevent the drill string from sticking during drilling operations. On the other hand, slick drill collars have a smooth outer surface and are used when there is no requirement for enhanced mud flow. Both types of drill collars are made from non-magnetic alloys to minimize magnetic interference and ensure accurate exploration during drilling activities.</p></p>
