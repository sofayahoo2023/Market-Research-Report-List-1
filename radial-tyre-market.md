<p><p>Radial tyres dominate the market due to their enhanced performance compared to bias-ply tyres. They are available in different types based on their construction materials. Whole steel wire radial tyres utilize steel wires for both the carcass and belts, offering excellent strength and stability. Half-steel wire radial tyres combine steel wires for belts and fabric for the carcass, providing a balance between strength and flexibility. Whole fiber radial tyres use high-strength synthetic fibers like rayon and polyester for both the carcass and belts, ensuring durability and a comfortable ride.</p></p>
