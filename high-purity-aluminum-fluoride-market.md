There are three types of high purity aluminum fluoride markets: wet aluminum fluoride, dry aluminum fluoride, and anhydrous aluminum fluoride. Wet aluminum fluoride is a form of the compound that contains moisture and is mainly used in the production of aluminum metal and aluminum alloys. Dry aluminum fluoride is a powdered form with low moisture content and is used in industries like ceramics. Anhydrous aluminum fluoride is a completely moisture-free form and is commonly used in aluminum smelting and as a catalyst.</p></p>
