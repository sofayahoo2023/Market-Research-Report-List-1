Medical freezers maintain temperatures between -10℃ and -25℃, ideal for preserving vaccines and other temperature-sensitive medications. Medical cryopreservation boxes create a controlled environment between -10℃ and -50℃, ensuring the safe storage of tissues and biological samples. Medical cryogenic storage boxes provide storage conditions ranging from -50℃ to -150℃, necessary for long-term preservation of organs and other biological materials. Liquid nitrogen tanks offer ultra-low temperatures between -150℃ and -196℃, supporting the preservation of biological samples. The "Others" category encompasses additional cryogenic equipment utilized in the medical industry.</p></p>
