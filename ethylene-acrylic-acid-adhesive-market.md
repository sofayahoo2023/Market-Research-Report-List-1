<p><p>Ethylene Acrylic Acid (EAA) Adhesive is a type of adhesive used in various industries. It comes in three different market types, namely Film Grade, Extrusion Coating Grade, and Dispersible Grade. Film Grade EAA adhesives are used for the lamination of films and provide excellent adhesion properties. Extrusion Coating Grade is commonly used in coatings for paper and packaging products. Dispersible Grade EAA adhesives are designed for use in water-based applications, such as paper coatings, where they can disperse easily in water without leaving any residue.</p></p>
