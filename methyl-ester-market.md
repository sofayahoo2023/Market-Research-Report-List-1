<p><p>Methyl ester, also known as biodiesel, is a renewable, clean-burning diesel substitute made from renewable sources such as vegetable oils and animal fats. Methyl ester is commonly used in transportation and industrial sectors as a blend with petroleum diesel or as a standalone fuel.</p><p>The methyl ester market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding in the coming years. One of the major drivers of this growth is the increasing demand for sustainable and eco-friendly fuels due to rising environmental concerns.
