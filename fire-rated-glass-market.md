<p><p>The fire-rated glass market consists of several types of fire-resistant glass, each catering to specific needs. Laminated fire-resistant glass is made by sandwiching a layer of fire-resistant material between two glass layers, providing protection against smoke and flames. Tempered fire-resistant glass undergoes a heating and cooling process, making it stronger and able to withstand high temperatures. Wired fire-resistant glass contains embedded wire mesh to prevent glass from falling when shattered. Ceramic fire-resistant glass is constructed by combining glass with ceramic layers, offering excellent resistance to fire and heat.</p></p>
