Heat pipes are heat transfer devices that are sealed containing an internal working fluid, usually a liquid with a low boiling point. <p><p>Heat pipe exchanger market is categorized into two main types. Type I heat pipe exchangers are primarily used for cooling purposes. They efficiently transfer heat from a high-temperature source to a low-temperature sink, ensuring effective cooling operations. On the other hand, Type II heat pipe exchangers are designed for heating purposes. They transfer heat from a high-temperature fluid to a lower temperature fluid, facilitating efficient heating operations. Both types of heat pipe exchangers play a significant role in various industrial applications by enabling effective heat transfer and energy optimization.</p></p>
