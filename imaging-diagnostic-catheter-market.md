<p><p>The imaging diagnostic catheter market consists of various types of catheters used in medical imaging procedures. Angiography catheters are specifically designed for angiographic studies to visualize blood vessels. Ultrasound catheters combine ultrasound technology with catheterization for detailed imaging of internal organs. OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) catheters enable cross-sectional images of tissues using light waves. Electrophysiology (EP) catheters are used for mapping and treating cardiac arrhythmias. Other imaging catheters include those used for intravascular ultrasound, intracardiac echocardiography, and fractional flow reserve measurements, among others.</p></p>
