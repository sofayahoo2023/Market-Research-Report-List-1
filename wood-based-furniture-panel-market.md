<p><p>The wood-based furniture panel market consists of various types such as particleboard (PB), medium-density fibreboard (MDF), high-density fibreboard (HDF), oriented strand board (OSB), and plywood. Particleboard is made by compressing wood particles with adhesive, while MDF is created by combining wood fibers with resin. HDF is a stronger version of MDF with higher density. OSB is made by compressing wood strands, and plywood is constructed by layering thin sheets of wood veneer. These different types cater to various applications and offer diverse strengths, durability, and price points in the furniture panel market.</p></p>
