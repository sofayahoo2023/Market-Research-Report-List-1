Manufacturers and consumers are increasingly opting for water-based and low-VOC (volatile organic compound) coatings to reduce environmental impact and comply with regulations. <p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Base Coat</li><li>Clearcoat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive plastic coating market consists of three main types: primer, base coat, and clearcoat. Primer is the initial layer applied to prepare the plastic surface for further coating and enhance adhesion. Base coat provides the color and texture to the plastic, serving as the main layer that gives the desired aesthetics. Clearcoat, the topmost layer, offers protection and adds glossiness to the plastic, ensuring durability and resistance to various environmental factors. Each type plays a vital role in providing a high-quality and appealing finish to automotive plastic surfaces.</p></p>
