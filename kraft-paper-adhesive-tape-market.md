<p><p>Kraft paper adhesive tape is available in two types: solvent-based and rubber-based. Solvent-based adhesive tapes are made by dissolving the adhesive in a solvent, which results in a strong and durable bond. These tapes offer excellent adhesion to a variety of surfaces and are suitable for applications that require long-lasting hold. On the other hand, rubber-based adhesive tapes have adhesive made from natural or synthetic rubber. They provide good tackiness and flexibility, making them easy to apply and remove. Rubber-based tapes are often used for packaging and sealing purposes.</p></p>
