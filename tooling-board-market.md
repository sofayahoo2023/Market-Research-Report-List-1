<p><p>Tooling board, also known as modeling board or tooling block, is a type of material used in the manufacturing industry for creating prototype models, molds, patterns, and various tooling applications. Tooling boards are commonly made from high-density polyurethane or epoxy resins, offering excellent dimensional stability, low thermal expansion, and high chemical resistance.</p><p>The future outlook of the tooling board market is expected to be highly positive. The growing demand for lightweight materials in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and marine, is one of the key factors driving the market's growth. Tooling boards are preferred for their lightweight properties, precision, and ability to replace traditional materials like wood or metal. Additionally, the increasing adoption of advanced manufacturing techniques like computer numerical control (CNC) machining and additive manufacturing is expected to boost the demand for tooling boards in the coming years.</p><p>Furthermore, the tooling board market is also supported by the rising demand for rapid prototyping and product development across industries.
