<p><p>The Racing Brake Fluid market consists of various types, namely DOT4, DOT5, and DOT5.1. DOT4 brake fluid is commonly used in high-performance cars and motorcycles due to its excellent performance under high temperatures. It has a boiling point of around 446°F (230°C) and absorbs less moisture, ensuring efficient braking even in extreme conditions. On the other hand, DOT5 brake fluid is silicone-based and has a higher boiling point than DOT4, making it suitable for racing applications that involve intense heat and stress. Lastly, DOT5.1 brake fluid is a glycol-based fluid offering superior performance and longer-lasting characteristics than DOT4, making it ideal for high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
