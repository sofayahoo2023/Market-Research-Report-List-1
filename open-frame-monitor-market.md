<p><p>The open frame monitor market consists of two main types: those with a touch screen and those without. Touch screen monitors can be further categorized into resistive or capacitive types. Resistive touch screens operate by detecting pressure applied to the screen, making them suitable for stylus or gloved use. On the other hand, capacitive touch screens rely on the conductive properties of the human body, offering a more sensitive and intuitive touch experience. Meanwhile, monitors without touch screen functionality solely display visual content and do not support touch-based interaction.</p></p>
