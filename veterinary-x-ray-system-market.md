<p><p>The veterinary X-ray system market consists of two main types: digital radiography and analog radiography. Digital radiography involves the use of electronic sensors to capture X-ray images, which are then displayed on a computer screen. This technology provides high-quality images that can be easily stored, shared, and manipulated. Analog radiography, on the other hand, uses traditional film-based X-ray machines, where the images are recorded on photographic film. Although analog radiography is less expensive initially, digital radiography is gaining popularity due to its superior image quality and convenience.</p></p>
