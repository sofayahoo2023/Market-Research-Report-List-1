<p><ul><li>Vane-type VVT</li><li>Gear-type VVT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable Valve Timing (VVT) systems in automotive engines are designed to optimize performance and fuel efficiency by allowing the valves to open and close at different times according to engine speed and load conditions. The Vane-type VVT system uses adjustable vanes to control the timing and lift of the valves. On the other hand, the Gear-type VVT system employs a hydraulically controlled camshaft gear to vary the valve timing. Both systems aim to enhance engine performance, reduce emissions, and maximize fuel economy.</p></p>
