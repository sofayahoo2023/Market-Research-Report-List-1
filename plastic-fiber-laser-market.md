<p><p>The Plastic Fiber Laser market consists of two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser and Pulsed Fiber Laser. CW Fiber Lasers produce a continuous beam of laser light, which is ideal for applications requiring steady and continuous energy delivery. On the other hand, Pulsed Fiber Lasers produce laser beams in short bursts or pulses, making them suitable for applications that require high peak power with shorter duration. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industrial sectors for cutting, welding, marking, and engraving plastic materials.</p></p>
