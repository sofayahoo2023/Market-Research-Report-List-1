<p><p>The eco-friendly tyre market is divided into two main types: all-steel and semi-steel. All-steel tyres are made with multiple steel belts, making them highly durable and resistant to punctures and wear. These tyres are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles, such as trucks and buses. On the other hand, semi-steel tyres combine steel belts with additional layers of fabric, offering a balance between durability and comfort. Semi-steel tyres are commonly used in passenger cars and provide a smooth and comfortable ride while still being environmentally friendly.</p></p>
