<p><ul><li>Battery-Operated Ignition</li><li>Magneto Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive ignition equipment market consists of two main types: battery-operated ignition and magneto systems. Battery-operated ignition utilizes the power from a vehicle's battery to generate a high-voltage spark, which ignites the fuel mixture in the engine. This system is commonly found in modern vehicles and provides a reliable source of ignition. On the other hand, magneto systems generate their own electricity using magnets, eliminating the need for a battery. Frequently used in older vehicles, these systems are known for their durability and resilience in harsh conditions.</p></p>
