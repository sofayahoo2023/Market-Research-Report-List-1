<p><p>The Active Railway Wheel Sensor market consists of two main types: Single Wheel Sensor and Double Wheel Sensor. A Single Wheel Sensor is a device that is installed near a single wheel of a railway vehicle to measure its speed, revolution count, and direction. On the other hand, a Double Wheel Sensor is designed to be placed between two wheels of a railway vehicle, allowing it to measure the speed, revolution count, and direction of both wheels simultaneously. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in monitoring and ensuring the safe and efficient operation of railway systems.</p></p>
