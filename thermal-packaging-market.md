<p><p>Thermal packaging market offers various types of solutions for temperature-sensitive products. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) packaging is lightweight, durable, and provides excellent insulation, commonly used for food and pharmaceutical products. Polyurethane packaging offers high insulation and is widely used for cold chain transportation of biopharmaceuticals. Vacuum Insulated Panels (VIP) offer advanced insulation properties and are suitable for higher temperature ranges. Natural fiber packaging utilizes sustainable materials such as jute or cellulose, offering biodegradable and recyclable options. Other types of thermal packaging include gel packs, phase change materials, and reflective insulation, catering to specific packaging requirements.</p></p>
