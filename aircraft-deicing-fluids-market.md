Type I fluids are designed for use in cold temperatures, effectively removing ice and frost from aircraft. Type II fluids have a thicker consistency and offer longer holdover times, making them suitable for snow and freezing precipitation conditions. Type IV fluids are the newest addition and are considered more environmentally friendly, as they are composed of non-toxic and biodegradable substances. These fluids efficiently remove ice, snow, and frost while minimizing the impact on the environment.</p></p>
