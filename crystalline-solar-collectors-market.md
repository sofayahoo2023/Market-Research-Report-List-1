<p><p>Crystalline solar collectors are used to capture and convert solar energy into usable electricity. There are three main types of collectors in the market. The flat plate collector consists of a blackened metal plate covered by a glass cover, which absorbs sunlight and transfers it as heat to a fluid. The evacuated tube collector comprises of multiple glass tubes with a vacuum layer, enhancing heat retention. Lastly, other types of collectors include integrated systems that combine both flat plate and evacuated tube technologies, offering improved energy production efficiency.</p></p>
