<p><p>Dry drum magnetic separators are used to separate and recover magnetic materials from a mixture or feed stream. These separators operate on the principle of a stationary magnet with alternate polarity, which creates an attractive magnetic field. As the material passes through the rotating drum, magnetic particles are attracted and collected on the surface of the drum, while non-magnetic particles pass through and are discharged. The collected magnetic materials can be further processed or utilized.</p><p>The dry drum magnetic separators market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
