<p><p>The griller system market is divided into two main types: electric grill and charcoal grill. An electric grill utilizes electricity to generate heat, offering a convenient and easy-to-use cooking option. It is suitable for indoor or outdoor cooking, as it produces minimal smoke. On the other hand, a charcoal grill relies on charcoal briquettes or lump charcoal for heat, providing a smoky flavor to the food. Charcoal grills are favored for their traditional grilling experience but require more effort in terms of lighting and temperature control.</p></p>
