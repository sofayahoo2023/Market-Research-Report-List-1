<p><p>The gasoline detergent market can be categorized into three main types: PIBA-Based, PEA-Based, and Others. PIBA-Based gasoline detergents are formulated using polyisobutene amine (PIBA), which helps in controlling deposits and prevents engine fouling. On the other hand, PEA-Based detergents use polyether amine (PEA) as an active ingredient to improve fuel economy and clean fuel injectors. Lastly, the Other category consists of gasoline detergents that utilize different ingredients or combinations to provide similar benefits as PIBA or PEA-based detergents.</p></p>
