<p><p>Polyamide-imide resin is a type of high-performance polymer used in various industries. There are two main types of polyamide-imide resin manufacturing routes: the acid chloride route and the diisocyanate route. The acid chloride route involves reacting an aromatic diamine with an aromatic acid chloride to form the polyamide-imide resin. On the other hand, the diisocyanate route involves reacting an aromatic diamine with an aromatic diisocyanate to obtain the resin. Both routes result in the production of polyamide-imide resin with excellent mechanical properties and high-temperature resistance.</p></p>
