<p><ul><li>RHF-α</li><li>RHF-β</li><li>Urofollitropin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recombinant human follitropin (RHF) market consists of two types: RHF-α and RHF-β. RHF-α is a synthetic form of human follicle-stimulating hormone (FSH) that helps stimulate the production of eggs in the ovaries. RHF-β, on the other hand, is a recombinant form of the beta subunit of FSH and is used in combination with RHF-α for in vitro fertilization treatments. Urofollitropin is another type of follicle-stimulating hormone derived from the urine of postmenopausal women, which is also used for female infertility treatment.</p></p>
