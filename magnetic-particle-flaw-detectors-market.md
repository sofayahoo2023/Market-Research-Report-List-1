<p><p>Magnetic Particle Flaw Detectors are non-destructive testing instruments used to detect surface or subsurface defects in ferromagnetic materials. They utilize magnetic fields and iron particles to identify flaws such as cracks, seams, or laps in various industries including automotive, aerospace, oil and gas, and manufacturing. The detectors work by creating a magnetic field in the material and applying iron particles on the surface. If there are any defects, the particles will gather at those areas and create a visible indication of the flaw.</p><p>The Magnetic Particle Flaw Detectors Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
