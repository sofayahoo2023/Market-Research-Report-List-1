<p><p>A triple offset butterfly valve is a type of industrial valve that is used to regulate and control the flow of fluids in various applications. It is called "triple offset" because of its unique design, which consists of three offsets or pivots from the valve centerline. This design allows the valve to achieve a bubble-tight closure and minimizes friction and wear, resulting in a longer service life.</p><p>The triple offset butterfly valve market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
