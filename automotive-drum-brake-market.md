<p><p>The automotive drum brake market is categorized into three types. Firstly, normal braking refers to the standard functioning of brakes when a driver presses the pedal to slow down or stop the vehicle. Secondly, automatic self-adjustment involves a mechanism within the brake system that constantly adjusts brake shoe clearance, ensuring efficient brake performance over time. Lastly, emergency braking refers to a situation where the brakes are applied abruptly and forcefully, typically when there is an immediate need to stop the vehicle to prevent a collision or any potential dangers.</p></p>
