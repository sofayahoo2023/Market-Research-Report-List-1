<p><p>The PLC Splitter Chip market includes two main types - 1xN and 2xN. In the 1xN market, the chip is designed to split the input signal into multiple output signals, represented by "N". For example, a 1x8 splitter chip splits one input signal into 8 output signals. In the 2xN market, the chip can combine multiple input signals and split them into multiple output signals. For instance, a 2x8 splitter chip can take two input signals and split them into 8 output signals each.</p></p>
