<p><p>In the market for robots used for cleaning photovoltaic panels, there are two main types - trackless and railed. Trackless robots are designed to move freely on the surface of the solar panels without requiring any fixed infrastructure. They use advanced sensors and navigation systems to move around efficiently. On the other hand, railed robots operate on a fixed rail system installed on top of the solar panels. These rail systems guide the robots' movement, ensuring precise cleaning along predefined paths. Both types have their advantages and are tailored to suit different cleaning requirements in the solar industry.</p></p>
