<p><p>The Automotive Lens market can be categorized into three types: Front View Lens, Rear/Surround View Lens, and Interior Vision Lens. Front View Lens refers to the lens used in front-facing cameras and sensors that provide visual information to the driver about the road ahead. Rear/Surround View Lens refers to lenses used in cameras around the vehicle to offer a 360-degree view for parking and maneuvering. Interior Vision Lens refers to lenses used inside the vehicle, such as in rear-view mirrors or driver monitoring systems, to provide enhanced visibility and monitor driver behavior.</p></p>
