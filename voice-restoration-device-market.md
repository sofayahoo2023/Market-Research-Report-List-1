<p><p>The Voice Restoration Device Market includes two main types: Electronic Larynx and Artificial Implant Throat. Electronic Larynx devices are portable and handheld devices used by individuals who have lost their voice due to conditions like cancer or laryngeal trauma. These devices generate sound waves that create speech when pressed against the throat. On the other hand, Artificial Implant Throat is a surgically implanted device that restores the voice by connecting the trachea and esophagus. Both types of devices aim to restore communication abilities in individuals with voice disorders.</p></p>
