<p><strong>The Sunflowerseed Meal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Kernel</li><li>Half Kernel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sunflowerseed meal is classified into two market types: whole kernel and half kernel. Whole kernel sunflowerseed meal is made from sunflower seeds that are not crushed or broken, preserving the intact kernel. This type of meal is mainly used in high-quality feed products and birdseed mixes. Half kernel sunflowerseed meal, on the other hand, is produced from crushed or partially broken sunflower seeds, resulting in a mixture of whole and broken kernels. This type of meal is commonly utilized in animal feed, as it provides a cost-effective protein source.</p></p>
