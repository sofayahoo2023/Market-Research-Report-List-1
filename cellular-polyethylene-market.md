<p><strong>The Cellular Polyethylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physically Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam</li><li>Chemically Cross-linked Polyethylene Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellular polyethylene market encompasses two main types: physically cross-linked polyethylene foam and chemically cross-linked polyethylene foam. Physically cross-linked polyethylene foam is created through a continuous extrusion process, resulting in a closed-cell structure that offers excellent insulation and cushioning properties. Chemically cross-linked polyethylene foam, on the other hand, is produced through a chemical reaction, resulting in a higher density foam with enhanced mechanical strength and heat resistance. Both types find applications in various industries, including packaging, automotive, construction, and marine.</p></p>
