<p><p>Electronic board level underfill and encapsulation materials are used to protect and enhance the reliability of electronic components on printed circuit boards (PCBs). The market includes various types such as no-flow underfill, capillary underfill, molded underfill, and wafer-level underfill. No-flow underfill is applied before the solder reflow process; capillary underfill flows between components through capillary action. Molded underfill is applied to the entire PCB and then molded together. Wafer-level underfill is applied during the semiconductor manufacturing process before dicing. Each type has specific applications and benefits in terms of protecting and strengthening electronic components.</p></p>
