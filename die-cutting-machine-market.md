<p><ul><li>Metal-to-Metal</li><li>Perfing</li><li>Slitting</li><li>Kiss Cutting</li><li>Scoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The die cutting machine market consists of various types of machines, each serving a specific purpose. Metal-to-metal die cutting machines are used for cutting shapes out of metal sheets. Perfing machines create perforated lines on materials, enabling easy tear-off. Slitting machines are used for cutting materials into narrow strips. Kiss cutting machines cut through the top layer of the material while leaving the backing intact. Scoring machines create crease lines on materials, facilitating precise folding. These machines cater to different industries and applications, offering versatility and efficiency in material cutting and processing tasks.</p></p>
