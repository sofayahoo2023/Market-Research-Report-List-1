<p><strong>The Automotive Engineering Plastics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Type</li><li>Thermoplastics Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive engineering plastics market consists of two main types: thermosetting type and thermoplastics type. </p><p>Thermosetting plastics are materials that undergo irreversible chemical reactions when heated, forming a rigid and infusible structure. They possess high heat resistance, excellent dimensional stability, and good electrical insulation properties. These plastics are used in components that require high durability and strength, such as engine parts, electrical connectors, and brake components.</p><p>On the other hand, thermoplastics are materials that can be melted and reshaped multiple times without changing their properties. They offer flexibility, impact resistance, and easy processability. These plastics are used in various automotive parts like interior trims, bumpers, dashboards, and exterior body panels.</p></p>
