<p><strong>The Fiber-digested Silage Inoculants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homofermentative</li><li>Heterofermentative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber-digested silage inoculants market can be classified into two types: homofermentative and heterofermentative. Homofermentative inoculants mainly consist of bacteria that produce lactic acid, resulting in enhanced fermentation and preservation of silage quality. They are preferred when maximum acidification is desirable. On the other hand, heterofermentative inoculants involve a mixture of various bacteria, producing both lactic acid and other fermentation byproducts. They offer benefits like improved aerobic stability and reduced spoilage, making them suitable for situations where reduced dry matter loss is essential.</p></p>
