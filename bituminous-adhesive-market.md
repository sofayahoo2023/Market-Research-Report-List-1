<p><p>Bituminous adhesive is a type of adhesive used in various industries, including construction and roofing. It is available in two forms: cold applied and hot applied. Cold applied bituminous adhesive is applied at ambient temperatures and does not require heating. It is easier to handle and has a longer shelf life. In contrast, hot applied bituminous adhesive is heated to high temperatures before application. This type of adhesive provides a stronger bond and is commonly used in heavy-duty applications. Both types of bituminous adhesive offer reliable adhesion and waterproofing properties.</p></p>
