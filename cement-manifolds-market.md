<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Cement manifolds are classified into two types: Simple Plug market and Double Plug market. Simple Plug market refers to a cement manifold system that utilizes a single plug to block the flow of cement during drilling operations. On the other hand, Double Plug market employs two plugs to isolate the cement slurry and create a seal to prevent mixing with drilling fluids. Both types are essential for ensuring efficient cementing processes and maintaining the integrity of oil and gas wells.</p></p>
