<p><p>A hydraulic press is a machine that uses hydraulic fluid to generate a compressive force. It consists of a piston, cylinder, and hydraulic fluid reservoir. When the hydraulic fluid is pumped into the cylinder, it causes the piston to move, thereby exerting a force on the object being pressed. Hydraulic presses are commonly used in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, for tasks such as metal forming, forging, and molding.</p><p>The hydraulic press market is experiencing steady growth due to several factors. Firstly, the rising demand for efficient and automated systems in manufacturing processes is driving the adoption of hydraulic presses.
