<p><p>The Car Night Vision System market includes Active Infrared Night Vision System and Passive Infrared Night Vision System types. Active Infrared Night Vision System uses infrared light beams emitted by the car to illuminate the road ahead and captures the reflected light to produce an image. On the other hand, Passive Infrared Night Vision System detects and captures the infrared radiation emitted by objects in the environment to create an image. Both types of systems enhance visibility in low light conditions and aid in preventing accidents while driving at night.</p></p>
