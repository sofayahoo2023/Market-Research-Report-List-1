<p><p>Plastics processing machinery refers to various types of machines used in the manufacturing and processing of plastic products. The Injection Molding Machine (IMM) is used to create precise and complex plastic parts by injecting molten plastic into a mold. The Extrusion Machine is used to produce long, continuous plastic parts such as pipes and tubes. The Blow Molding Machine (BMM) is utilized to create hollow plastic products by inflating molten plastic in a mold. The "Other" market segment includes different types of plastic processing machines like rotation molding and thermoforming machines.</p></p>
