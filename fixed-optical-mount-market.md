<p><p>The Fixed Optical Mount Market includes two main types: Cage Mountable and Post Mountable. Cage Mountable refers to an optical mount that can be attached to a cage system. It provides flexibility and easy alignment adjustments due to its adaptability with other components within the cage structure. On the other hand, Post Mountable optical mounts are designed to be directly mounted on a post or post holder. They offer stability and precise positioning due to their fixed attachment to a vertical support structure. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of optical system users.</p></p>
