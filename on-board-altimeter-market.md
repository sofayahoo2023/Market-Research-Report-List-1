<p><p>The on-board altimeter market comprises various types of altimeters used for measuring altitude in different applications. Barometric altimeters use atmospheric pressure to determine altitude and are commonly used in aviation and weather monitoring. Radio altimeters measure altitude based on radio waves reflected from the ground and are widely used in aircraft for landing purposes. GNSS altimeters utilize satellite positioning systems like GPS and Galileo to determine altitude accurately. Laser altimeters use laser beams to measure vertical distances in applications like surveying and forestry. Other types of altimeters may include radar altimeters and sonar altimeters.</p></p>
