<p><p>The 3D laser cutting machine market can be classified into three types: fiber laser cutting machines, CO2 laser cutting machines, and solid-state laser cutting machines. Fiber laser cutting machines utilize a fiber laser source to deliver high power, precision, and speed for cutting various materials. CO2 laser cutting machines use a gas laser source to provide versatility and flexibility for cutting a wide range of materials. Solid-state laser cutting machines employ a solid-state laser beam source to offer high stability and efficiency for precision cutting tasks.</p></p>
