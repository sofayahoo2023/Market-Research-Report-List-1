Hemicellulose is a polysaccharide found in plant cell walls that is used for its gel-forming and gelling abilities. Pectin is another polysaccharide found in fruits and vegetables and is used for its gelling and thickening properties.</p><p>Exudate gums are natural gums obtained from plant exudates such as agar, gum arabic, and gum tragacanth. These gums are used for their adhesive and thickening properties.</p><p>Lastly, the "other" market category includes animal source hydrocolloids that do not fit into the aforementioned categories.
