<p><p>The high-temperature camera system market consists of two main types: real vision camera systems and thermographic vision camera systems. Real vision camera systems are designed to capture images or videos in high temperatures, providing real-time visualization and monitoring of processes in industries such as manufacturing or power generation. On the other hand, thermographic vision camera systems focus on capturing thermal data, allowing users to detect temperature variations and identify potential issues like overheating in equipment or structures. Both types of systems play crucial roles in enhancing safety and efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
