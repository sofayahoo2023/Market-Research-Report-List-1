<p><p>A First In First Out (FIFO) rack is a storage system that is designed to optimize inventory management by ensuring that the oldest items are used or sold first. It is primarily used in warehouses or distribution centers where it is crucial to maintain stock rotation and minimize waste. The system works by loading new inventory from one end of the rack while allowing the older inventory to be retrieved from the opposite end.</p><p>The FIFO rack market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. <p><p>Selective Pallet Rack is a market type that allows for easy access to any pallet in the system, as it operates on the principle of first in, first out. Gravity Flow Rack also follows the FIFO principle, but is designed to effortlessly move products along inclined rollers or wheels. This facilitates quick and efficient inventory turnover. Pass-through Rack is another FIFO market type that offers access to products from both sides of the system, perfect for high-volume warehouses requiring continuous product rotation. Overall, these market types prioritize efficient stock rotation and accessibility.</p></p>
