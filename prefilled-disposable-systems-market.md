<p><strong>The Prefilled Disposable Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simple Chamber</li><li>Dual Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prefilled disposable systems market includes simple chamber and dual chamber types. In simple chamber systems, a single chamber is used to store the medication or substance, which is then ready for immediate use. On the other hand, dual chamber systems have two separate chambers, one for storing the active ingredient and the other for storing the diluent or solvent. These two chambers are mixed together at the time of administration to ensure the stability and effectiveness of the medication. Both types provide convenience and safety for single-use applications in various industries.</p></p>
