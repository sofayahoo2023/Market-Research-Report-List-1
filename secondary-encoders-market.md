<p><strong>The Secondary Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Encoders</li><li>Incremental Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Secondary encoders are devices used in various industries to provide accurate position feedback. Absolute encoders provide a unique digital code for each position, allowing for precise measurements and eliminating the need for a reference point. Incremental encoders, on the other hand, output pulses for each incremental movement, requiring a reference point for determining absolute position. Both types play a crucial role in motion control systems, robotics, and automation, offering reliable and accurate position feedback for improved precision and control.</p></p>
