<p><p>The Blood Glucose Monitors market consists of two main types: Blood Glucose Test Strips and Blood Glucose Meters. Blood Glucose Test Strips are used to collect a blood sample and test the user's glucose levels. They are often disposable and require pairing with a compatible Blood Glucose Meter for accurate results. On the other hand, Blood Glucose Meters are electronic devices that measure the glucose levels in the blood sample from the test strip. They display the results on a screen, allowing users to monitor their glucose levels easily.</p></p>
