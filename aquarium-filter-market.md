<p><p>The aquarium filter market can be classified into two main types: freshwater and saltwater. Freshwater filters are designed specifically to cater to the needs of aquariums that house freshwater fish species. These filters employ various techniques, such as mechanical, biological, and chemical filtration, to maintain optimal water quality. On the other hand, saltwater filters are specially designed for aquariums that house saltwater fish species and corals. These filters often incorporate additional features like protein skimmers to remove organic waste and maintain the delicate balance of a saltwater aquarium.</p></p>
