<p><strong>The Furniture Lacquer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The furniture lacquer market can be classified into two types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based lacquers use organic solvents as a base, which evaporate during the drying process, leaving behind a hard and durable finish. They are known for their quick drying time and excellent resistance to chemicals and abrasion. On the other hand, water-based lacquers use water as a base, making them eco-friendly and low in VOCs. They are preferred by those who prioritize environmental sustainability and easy cleanup. Both types offer different advantages and are used based on individual requirements and preferences.</p></p>
