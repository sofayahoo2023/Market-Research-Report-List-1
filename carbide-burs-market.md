<p><p>The carbide burs market can be categorized into two types: single-use and multi-use. Single-use carbide burs are designed to be used only once and then discarded. They offer the advantage of being sterile and ensuring a fresh cutting surface for each use, making them preferred in medical and dental applications. On the other hand, multi-use carbide burs are designed to be sterilized and reused multiple times. These burs are more cost-effective in the long run as they can be cleaned and maintained, extending their lifespan before replacement is needed.</p></p>
