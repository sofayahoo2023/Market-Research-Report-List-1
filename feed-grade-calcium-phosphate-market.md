<p><p>Feed grade calcium phosphate is a type of mineral supplement commonly used in animal feed. There are four main types: Monocalcium Phosphate (MCP), Dicalcium Phosphate (DCP), Mono-Dicalcium Phosphate (MDCP), and Tricalcium Phosphate (TCP). MCP is often used in poultry and swine feed for its high phosphorus content. DCP is widely used in livestock feed as it provides both phosphorus and calcium. MDCP is a mixture of MCP and DCP and is used in various animal diets. TCP is primarily used in ruminant feed to ensure proper bone development and milk production.</p></p>
