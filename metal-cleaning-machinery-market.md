Aqueous metal cleaning equipment, on the other hand, uses water-based cleaning solutions. These solutions are environmentally friendly and remove dirt, oils, and other impurities using the power of water and detergents. Both types of equipment cater to the specific cleaning needs of industries that deal with metal surfaces.</p></p>
