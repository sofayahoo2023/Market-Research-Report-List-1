<p><ul><li>Conventional Ligation</li><li>Self-Ligating Brackets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aesthetic orthodontic bracket market includes two main types: conventional ligation brackets and self-ligating brackets. Conventional ligation brackets require the use of elastic or metal ties to hold the archwire in place, while self-ligating brackets have a built-in mechanism to hold the archwire without the need for ties. Conventional brackets provide versatility but can be bulkier and less aesthetically pleasing. Self-ligating brackets are more discreet, with lower friction and potential for faster treatment, but they are also more expensive. Both types offer options for patients seeking orthodontic treatment with aesthetic considerations.</p></p>
