<p><p>The lawn pruning machinery market consists of different types of lawn mowers designed for various purposes. Hobbing lawn mowers feature a reel that cuts the grass by shearing it against a stationary cutter bar. Rotary lawn mowers use a rotating blade underneath to cut the grass. Scissors lawn mowers have a scissor-like action, where the blades move back and forth to cut the grass. These different types cater to different lawn maintenance needs, providing options for users to choose the most suitable machinery for their specific requirements.</p></p>
