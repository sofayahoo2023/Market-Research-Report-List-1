<p><strong>The Enterprise Digital Rights Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise Digital Rights Management (EDRM) market types can be categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I EDRM market is focused on protecting sensitive data within an organization by providing robust access controls, encryption, and audit trails. It ensures the confidentiality and integrity of digital assets. On the other hand, Type II EDRM market extends beyond internal data protection and focuses on securing information shared with external partners, customers, and suppliers. It includes features like secure document sharing, watermarking, and rights management across multiple organizations. Both types address different aspects of digital rights management but serve the purpose of safeguarding data.</p></p>
