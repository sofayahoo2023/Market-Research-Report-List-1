<p><p>The semiconductor chemical filter market offers various types of filters such as PP membrane, PES membrane, PTFE membrane, PE membrane, Nylon66 membrane, and others. PP membrane filters are made from polypropylene and are commonly used in applications involving aqueous solutions. PES membrane filters are made from polyethersulfone and are preferred for their higher chemical resistance. PTFE membrane filters are constructed from polytetrafluoroethylene and provide excellent chemical resistance and low extractable levels. PE membrane filters are made from polyethylene and are used for general filtration purposes. Nylon66 membrane filters are made from nylon and offer high mechanical strength and thermal stability. The "Others" category includes various membrane filters that don't fit into the mentioned types.</p></p>
