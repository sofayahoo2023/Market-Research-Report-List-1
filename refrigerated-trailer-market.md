<p><p>The refrigerated trailer market is divided into two main types: frozen transport and chilled transport. In frozen transport, these trailers are used to transport goods that need to be kept at extremely low temperatures, typically below freezing point. This includes products such as ice cream, frozen meats, and seafood. On the other hand, chilled transport involves trailers that maintain a cool temperature but above freezing point, typically between 0 to 5 degrees Celsius. This type of transport is often used for perishable items like fruits, vegetables, dairy products, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
