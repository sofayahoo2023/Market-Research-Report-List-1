<p><p>Silicone molding compounds market is categorized into two main types: press molding and injection molding. Press molding involves compressing the compound into a mold under high pressure and temperature, creating a solid product. This method is commonly used for manufacturing simple products with low complexity. On the other hand, injection molding uses an injection machine to inject the compound into a mold, allowing for the production of complex shapes and designs. This method is widely used in various industries due to its versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
