<p><p>Fiber test equipment market consists of two primary types, namely portable/handheld and rack-mounted/benchtop. Portable or handheld equipment is compact, lightweight, and designed for field technicians to conveniently perform tests on the go. These devices are battery-powered and offer ease of use and portability. On the other hand, rack-mounted or benchtop equipment is larger in size and typically used in laboratories or fixed locations. They provide extended capabilities for complex testing, multiple fiber connections, and advanced functionalities, making them suitable for more extensive and sophisticated fiber testing requirements.</p></p>
