<p><p>The distal radius plates and screws market comprises two main types of devices: distal radius plates and distal radius screws. Distal radius plates are anatomically contoured plates used to stabilize fractures or other injuries near the wrist joint. They are typically made of titanium or stainless steel and are available in various shapes and sizes to accommodate different patient anatomies. Distal radius screws, on the other hand, are small screws used in conjunction with plates to achieve optimal stabilization. Both devices are commonly used in orthopedic surgeries to treat wrist fractures and other related conditions.</p></p>
