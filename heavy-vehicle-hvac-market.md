<p><p>The Heavy Vehicle HVAC market includes two main types of systems: Engine Powered HVAC and Electric Powered HVAC. Engine Powered HVAC systems rely on the vehicle's engine to generate power, using a belt-driven compressor to cool or heat the cabin. On the other hand, Electric Powered HVAC systems utilize electric power from the vehicle's battery or external power source. These systems typically have a separate electric compressor and are more energy-efficient, providing a greener alternative. Both types aim to offer comfortable temperature control for heavy vehicle cabins.</p></p>
