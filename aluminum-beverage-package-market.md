<p><p>The aluminum beverage packaging market consists of two types: three-piece and two-piece packaging. Three-piece packaging refers to aluminum cans made from three separate components - the top lid, the body, and the bottom end. These components are individually manufactured and then assembled. On the other hand, two-piece packaging involves the production of aluminum cans using only two components - the body and the top lid, which are manufactured simultaneously as a single unit. Both types of packaging have their own advantages and are used extensively in the beverage industry.</p></p>
