<p><p>In-Line Planetary Gearboxes are compact and highly efficient gearboxes that are used to transfer power from a motor to an output shaft. They are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, robotics, and machinery.</p><p>These gearboxes consist of a central sun gear, multiple planetary gears, and an outer ring gear. The power is transferred through the meshing of these gears, resulting in high torque and precision.
