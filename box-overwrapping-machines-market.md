<p><p>Box overwrapping machines are used in various industries to wrap and seal boxes securely before shipping or storage. The market for these machines can be categorized into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. </p><p>Manual machines require human intervention for every step of the wrapping process, including feeding the boxes, applying the wrapping material, and sealing them. Semi-automatic machines automate some of these steps, but still require human assistance in certain stages. Automatic machines, on the other hand, are fully automated and can perform the wrapping process without any human intervention. These different types cater to the diverse needs and capacities of businesses in terms of production volume and workforce availability.</p></p>
