<p><p>Dunnage air bags are widely used for securing and cushioning cargo during transportation. Poly-woven bags are made from woven polypropylene fabric, providing durability and resistance to moisture. Kraft paper bags, on the other hand, are made from reinforced paper for improved strength. Vinyl bags are made from PVC material and offer high puncture resistance.
