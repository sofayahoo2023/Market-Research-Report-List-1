<p><strong>The Elastic Nonwovens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbonded Fabric</li><li>Staples Fabric</li><li>Melt Blown Fabric</li><li>Composite Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elastic nonwovens market is divided into several types of fabrics: spunbonded fabric, staples fabric, melt blown fabric, and composite fabric. Spunbonded fabric refers to a nonwoven material made by spinning and bonding polypropylene fibers, providing elasticity and durability. Staples fabric involves intertwining short fibers together, creating a soft and absorbent material. Melt blown fabric is produced by melting and extruding polymers through tiny nozzles to form microfibers, suitable for filtration applications. Meanwhile, composite fabric combines different nonwoven fabrics through laminating or bonding processes, offering versatile properties for various uses.</p></p>
