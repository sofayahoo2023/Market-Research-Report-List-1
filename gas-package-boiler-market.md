<p><p>The gas package boiler market is segmented into three types: D-type package boilers, A-type package boilers, and O-type package boilers. D-type package boilers have a large furnace area and are ideal for high-pressure and superheated steam applications. A-type package boilers have a small water and steam capacity and are commonly used for industrial processes. O-type package boilers have a unique design, with steam and water circulating in opposite directions, making them highly efficient for high-pressure applications. These boilers provide various options for users based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
