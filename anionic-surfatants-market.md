<p><p>Anionic surfactants are organic compounds commonly used in various industries for their excellent detergent properties. They possess a negatively charged hydrophilic group, usually a sulfate or sulfonate, balanced by a hydrophobic hydrocarbon chain. This combination allows anionic surfactants to act as excellent foaming agents, emulsifiers, and wetting agents. They are widely used in applications such as household cleaning, personal care products, petroleum recovery, textiles, and others.</p><p>The global anionic surfactants market is set to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
