<p><p>The passenger car air filter market is segmented into two types: intake air filter and cabin air filter. The intake air filter, also known as the engine air filter, is designed to clean the air that enters the car's engine, preventing dust, dirt, and other particles from damaging the engine components. On the other hand, the cabin air filter, also called the pollen filter, purifies the air that enters the passenger compartment, ensuring a clean and healthy environment for the occupants by trapping dust, pollen, and other pollutants present in the outside air.</p></p>
