<p><p>Vibration energy harvesting systems market can be categorized into three types: linear systems, nonlinear systems, and rotational systems. Linear systems capture energy from vibrations with a single direction of motion, such as those generated by machines or vehicles. Nonlinear systems collect energy from vibrations with multiple directions, such as those found in human movements. Rotational systems harvest energy from vibrations caused by rotating objects, such as in industrial machinery. These systems enable the conversion of mechanical vibrations into usable electrical energy for various applications.</p></p>
