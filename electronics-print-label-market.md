<p><p>The Electronics Print Label market consists of various types of labels used in the electronics industry. Wet Glued Labels are paper-based labels applied using adhesives. Pressure Sensitive/Self Adhesive Labels have a sticky backing for easy application. Liner-less labels are self-adhesive labels without a backing liner. Multi-part Barcode Labels are used for tracking and inventory purposes. In-mold labels are integrated into plastic products during their manufacturing process. Shrink Sleeve Labels are heat-sensitive PVC or PET labels that are applied around containers and shrink to fit tightly.</p></p>
