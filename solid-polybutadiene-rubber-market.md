<p><p>Solid polybutadiene rubber is a type of synthetic rubber that is widely used in various industries. There are different types of solid polybutadiene rubber available in the market.</p><p>High cis solid polybutadiene rubber has a high level of cis-1,4 polymerization, which results in high elasticity, tear strength, and abrasion resistance. It is commonly used in the production of tires, shoe soles, and conveyor belts.</p><p>Low cis solid polybutadiene rubber has a lower cis-1,4 polymerization level, resulting in lower elasticity and better processability. It is often used in the manufacturing of molded products, such as rubber seals and gaskets.</p><p>High trans solid polybutadiene rubber has a high level of trans-1,4 polymerization, which gives it high heat resistance and low air permeability. This type of rubber is often utilized in the production of rubber linings for hoses and tanks, as well as electrical insulation.</p><p>High vinyl solid polybutadiene rubber has a high vinyl content, providing it with excellent adhesion properties. It is commonly used as an adhesive in the bonding of rubber to metal or other substrates, making it suitable for applications such as automotive parts and industrial products.</p></p>
