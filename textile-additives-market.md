<p><strong>The Textile Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Textile additives market is categorized into two main types, Type I and Type II. Type I market refers to the additives used for enhancing the performance and properties of fibers, such as colorants, UV stabilizers, flame retardants, etc. These additives play a crucial role in improving the durability, safety, and appearance of textiles. On the other hand, Type II market involves additives used for fabric finishes, including softeners, water repellents, anti-static agents, etc. These additives are applied after fabric manufacturing to provide specific functionalities like smoothness, moisture resistance, and static control.</p></p>
