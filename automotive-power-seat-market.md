<p><p>The automotive power seat market includes two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic seats. Fully automatic seats are equipped with advanced functionalities that allow users to adjust the seat position, backrest angle, lumbar support, and headrest position with the push of a button. They often come with memory settings that can store personalized preferences for multiple users. On the other hand, semi-automatic seats offer some level of manual control in addition to basic motorized functionality. While they have motorized adjustments, users may need to manually fine-tune the seat position or backrest angle.</p></p>
