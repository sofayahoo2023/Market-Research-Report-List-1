<p><p>Textile machine lubricants are categorized into two types: mineral oil based and synthetic oil based. Mineral oil based lubricants are derived from petroleum and provide excellent lubrication, heat dissipation, and corrosion protection. They are commonly used in textile machines due to their affordability and availability. On the other hand, synthetic oil based lubricants are artificially produced using chemicals. They have superior lubricating properties, thermal stability, and resistance to oxidation, making them suitable for high-performance textile machinery applications.</p></p>
