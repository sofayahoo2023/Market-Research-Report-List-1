Argon gas is commonly used for TIG welding processes, providing excellent arc stability and weld quality. Carbon dioxide gas is mainly utilized in MIG welding due to its ability to penetrate deeply, making it suitable for welding thick materials. Oxygen gas is used in a variety of welding applications, improving heating and metal oxidation. Hydrogen gas is employed for specialized welding processes, such as atomic hydrogen welding, offering high heat intensity.</p></p>
