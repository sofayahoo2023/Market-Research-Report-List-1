<p><p>Photoionization Detection (PID) Sensors and Detectors are used to detect and measure volatile organic compounds (VOCs) in the air. Fixed PID sensors and detectors are permanently installed in specific locations to continuously monitor VOC levels. Portable PID sensors and detectors, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be easily carried around for on-site monitoring. Both types utilize UV lights to ionize VOCs, which then create detectable electrical currents. These sensors and detectors are crucial for ensuring air quality in various industries such as manufacturing, environmental monitoring, and occupational safety.</p></p>
