<p><ul><li>Combiner</li><li>Windshield</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive 3-D HUD (Heads-Up Display) technology offers two market types: Combiner and Windshield. Combiner HUD projects information onto a transparent panel situated in the driver's line of sight, blending graphics with the real-world view. This panel is typically located near the windshield. On the other hand, Windshield HUD projects information directly onto the windshield, superimposing graphics onto the driver's view without the need for a separate transparent panel. Both technologies aim to provide drivers with crucial information while minimizing distractions and enhancing safety on the road.</p></p>
