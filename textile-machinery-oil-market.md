<p><strong>The Textile Machinery Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coning Oil</li><li>Knitting Oil</li><li>Greases</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Textile Machinery Oil refers to various lubricants used in textile industry to ensure smooth operation and better performance of textile machinery. Coning Oil is specifically designed for cone winding machines and helps in reducing friction and wear between the yarn and the machine parts. Knitting Oil is used in knitting machines to lubricate needles and sinkers for improved efficiency and reduced breakages. Greases are thick lubricants used in various textile machinery components for long-lasting lubrication and protection against corrosion. Other market types include oils used for looms, spinning machines, and other textile machinery parts to provide optimal lubrication and prevent overheating.</p></p>
