<p><p>The Modified Polypropylene Material market is divided into two types: Physical Modification and Chemical Modification. Physical Modification refers to the alteration of polypropylene material by changing its physical properties such as melting point, tensile strength, and impact resistance through methods like blending, addition of fillers, or melt processing techniques. On the other hand, Chemical Modification involves changing the chemical structure and properties of polypropylene material by introducing additives, cross-linking agents, or using reactive polymers to enhance characteristics like heat resistance, flame retardancy, or electrical conductivity.</p></p>
