<p><p>The Smart Irrigation Systems market can be categorized into three types: Weather-Based Controllers, Sensor-Based Controllers, and Others. Sensor-Based Controllers use various sensors, like soil moisture sensors, to monitor real-time environmental conditions and optimize irrigation based on the actual needs of the plants.
