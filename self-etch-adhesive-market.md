<p><p>The self-etch adhesive market consists of two main types: single component and two component adhesives. Single component adhesives are ready to use and require no mixing, making them more convenient and time-saving. They typically contain a mixture of acidic monomers, solvents, and initiators. In contrast, two component adhesives require mixing of separate components before application. These adhesives generally consist of a primer and a bonding agent, chosen for their specific chemical properties. Two component adhesives provide superior bonding strength and durability compared to single component adhesives.</p></p>
