<p><p>Smart nanomaterials market is classified into various types based on their composition. Metal-based smart nanomaterials are developed using metals such as gold, silver, or iron and possess properties like enhanced conductivity and catalytic activity. Carbon-based smart nanomaterials are made of carbon compounds such as graphene or carbon nanotubes, offering exceptional mechanical strength and electrical conductivity. Polymeric smart nanomaterials are composed of polymers and can exhibit shape-memory, self-healing, and stimuli-responsiveness properties. Other types include hybrid nanomaterials and bio-inspired nanomaterials that combine different compositions for multifunctionality and mimic biological systems, respectively.</p></p>
