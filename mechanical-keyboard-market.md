<p><p>The mechanical keyboard market consists of three types of switches: linear switches, tactile non-clicky switches, and clicky switches. Linear switches provide a smooth and consistent keystroke with no tactile bump or click sound. Tactile non-clicky switches have a noticeable bump during keystrokes but do not produce clicky sounds. Clicky switches, on the other hand, offer both a tactile bump and an audible click to enhance the typing experience. These different switch types cater to various user preferences in terms of key feel and sound feedback.</p></p>
