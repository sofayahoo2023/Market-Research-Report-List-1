<p><p>A backboard stretcher, also known as a spinal board or backboard, is a medical device used for immobilizing and transporting individuals with suspected spinal cord injuries. The backboard provides stability to the spine and minimizes movement, reducing the risk of further damage.</p><p>The current outlook of the backboard stretcher market is positive, as there is a growing demand for efficient and safe patient transportation. With increasing awareness about the importance of spinal immobilization during emergencies, the usage of backboard stretchers is expected to rise.
