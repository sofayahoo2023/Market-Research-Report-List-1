The second type is spherical structure radomes, which have a rounded shape resembling a sphere and provide protection to the antenna. The third type comprises other structures that do not fall into the aforementioned categories, such as flat panel radomes. These different types of radomes cater to various antenna systems' requirements and can be used in a wide range of commercial applications.</p></p>
