<p><p>The multifunctional dental chair market includes two types: air control dental chairs and electronic control dental chairs. Air control dental chairs are operated using air pressure and typically feature manual controls for chair positioning, backrest movement, and headrest adjustment. On the other hand, electronic control dental chairs are equipped with electric motors and offer automated controls for various chair functions, allowing dentists to easily adjust the chair, backrest, headrest, and footrest. Both types of chairs serve the purpose of providing comfort and convenience to patients during dental procedures.</p></p>
