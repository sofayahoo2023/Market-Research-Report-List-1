<p><strong>The Truck Driving Recorder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated</li><li>Portable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The integrated truck driving recorder market refers to devices that are built-in or integrated into the truck's system. These recorders are permanently installed and provide continuous monitoring and recording of various parameters such as speed, location, and driver behavior. They are typically linked to the truck's telematics system for real-time data transmission and analysis. </p><p>On the other hand, the portable truck driving recorder market consists of devices that can be easily installed and removed from different trucks. These recorders are usually smaller and more lightweight, allowing for flexibility in their usage. They offer similar functions as integrated recorders but provide the advantage of being transferable between vehicles.</p></p>
