There are two main types of empty capsules on the market: gelatin capsules and non-gelatin capsules. Gelatin capsules are made from animal-derived gelatin and are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and suitability for a wide range of applications. Non-gelatin capsules, on the other hand, are made from plant-based materials or synthetic polymers and offer alternatives for those with religious, dietary, or cultural restrictions on animal-derived products.
