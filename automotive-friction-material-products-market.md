<p><p>The automotive friction material products market consists of three main types: brake linings, brake pads, and clutch facings. Brake linings are designed to provide friction and stop the vehicle when the brakes are applied. Brake pads, on the other hand, are a more modern alternative and are made of a stronger material that produces less noise and dust. Lastly, clutch facings provide the necessary friction to engage or disengage the clutch and change gears smoothly in manual transmission vehicles.</p></p>
