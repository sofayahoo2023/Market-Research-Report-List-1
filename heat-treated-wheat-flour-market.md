<p><p>Heat-treated wheat flour is a type of flour that undergoes a thermal treatment process to improve its functionality, nutritional profile, and extend its shelf life. It also leads to increased solubility and hydration properties, resulting in superior functionality compared to regular wheat flour.</p><p>The heat-treated wheat flour market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Secondly, the increasing demand for convenience food products, such as bakery and confectionery, is boosting the market growth as heat-treated flour offers improved dough handling, texture, and shelf stability, making it suitable for various food applications.
