<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The connected car market consists of three main types: embedded solutions, integrated solutions, and tethered solutions. Embedded solutions involve the car manufacturer integrating connectivity features into the vehicle during the manufacturing process. Integrated solutions enable connectivity by pairing the car with other devices, like smartphones or wearable technology. Tethered solutions require a physical connection, such as a cable or Wi-Fi hotspot, to access connectivity features in the car. These different types cater to various consumer needs and preferences, offering a range of options for staying connected on the road.</p></p>
