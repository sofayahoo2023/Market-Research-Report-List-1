Chronic wounds, on the other hand, are long-lasting wounds that may be difficult to heal, such as pressure ulcers or diabetic foot ulcers. Surgical wounds are incisions made during surgeries that require effective protection and healing. Medical barrier film products provide a protective layer on these wounds, preventing infections, promoting healing, and maintaining a moist environment conducive to tissue regeneration.</p></p>
