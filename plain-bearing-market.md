<p><p>Plain bearings, also known as sleeve bearings or bushings, are used to reduce friction and enable smooth rotational or linear movement in various machines and equipment. Journal bearings are cylindrical in shape and provide rotational support for a shaft within a housing. Linear bearings facilitate linear motion along a track or guide rail. Thrust bearings support axial loads and prevent axial movement. Angular contact bearings accommodate both radial and axial loads simultaneously, enabling rotation at high speeds. All these types of plain bearings are essential components in different mechanical systems for efficient and reliable operation.</p></p>
