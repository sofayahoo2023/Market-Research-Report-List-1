<p><ul><li>Offline Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment</li><li>Inline Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D AOI Equipment Market consists of two major types: Offline Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment and Inline Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment. Offline AOI Equipment is used for inspecting printed circuit boards (PCBs) after the manufacturing process is complete. It allows for thorough and detailed inspection, but at a slower pace. On the other hand, Inline AOI Equipment is integrated directly into the assembly line, providing real-time inspection as the PCBs are being produced. This enables faster inspection and immediate feedback, ensuring higher efficiency and productivity in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
