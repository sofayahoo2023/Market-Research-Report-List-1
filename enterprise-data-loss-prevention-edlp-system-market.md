<p><p>Enterprise Data Loss Prevention (EDLP) systems are designed to protect sensitive data within organizations. These systems can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises.</p><p>Cloud-based EDLP systems rely on cloud infrastructure to store and process data. This type offers advantages such as flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness.
