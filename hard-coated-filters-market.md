<p><p>The hard-coated filters market includes different types of filters, such as longpass filters and shortpass filters. A longpass filter is designed to transmit light with longer wavelengths while blocking shorter wavelengths. It allows only longer wavelengths to pass through the filter. On the other hand, a shortpass filter transmits shorter wavelengths while blocking longer wavelengths. It allows only shorter wavelengths to pass through the filter. These filters find applications in various industries where light filtering is necessary, like photography, microscopy, and spectroscopy.</p></p>
