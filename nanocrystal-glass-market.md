<p><p>Nanocrystal glass can be classified into two main types: single crystal and multi crystal market. Single crystal nanocrystal glass refers to glass that is composed of a single crystal of nanocrystals, providing high crystallinity and uniformity. This type of glass is often used in advanced electronic devices, optical applications, and energy storage. On the other hand, multi crystal nanocrystal glass consists of multiple crystals that are fused together. This type of glass offers a higher degree of flexibility, making it suitable for applications like flexible displays and wearable devices.</p></p>
