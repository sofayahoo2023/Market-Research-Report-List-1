<p><p>The Nuclear Moisture Separator Reheater Vessels (MSR) market is categorized into two types: Horizontal MSR and Vertical MSR. The Horizontal MSR is a type of vessel where steam flows horizontally, and the moisture separation and reheating process occur in a horizontal direction. On the other hand, the Vertical MSR is a vessel where steam flows vertically, and the moisture separation and reheating process take place vertically. These two types offer different orientations for the steam flow and provide options for the implementation of moisture separation and reheating in nuclear power plants.  Overall, the market offers choices between horizontal and vertical configurations of MSR vessels for nuclear power plants.</p></p>
