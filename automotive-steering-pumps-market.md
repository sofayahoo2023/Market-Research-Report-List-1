<p><p>Automotive Steering Pumps Market Types:</p><p>1. Mechanical Hydraulic Power Steering: This type of steering system uses a mechanical pump to generate hydraulic pressure, which assists the driver in turning the wheels. It requires mechanical linkage between the steering wheel and the wheels and is commonly found in older vehicles.</p><p>2. Electronic Hydraulic Power Steering: This type combines electronics with hydraulic power steering. It uses sensors and control modules to monitor the driver's input and adjust the hydraulic pressure accordingly, providing a smoother and more precise steering experience.</p><p>3. Electric Power Steering: This system eliminates the need for hydraulic systems altogether by using an electric motor to assist the steering. It offers greater fuel efficiency and flexibility in terms of adjustment and control, as it can be easily integrated with other vehicle systems.</p></p>
