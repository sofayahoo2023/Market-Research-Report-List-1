<p><p>The current sampling resistance market consists of three main types: thick film, thin film, and metal plate. Thick film resistors are made by screen printing a resistive paste onto a substrate, offering high power handling capabilities and cost-effective production. Thin film resistors are made by depositing a thin layer of resistive material onto a substrate, providing higher precision and stability but at a higher cost. Metal plate resistors are made by cutting and shaping metal sheets, offering excellent power handling and stability but limited precision. These different types cater to various applications and cost requirements.</p></p>
